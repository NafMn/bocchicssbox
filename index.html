Bocchi the Rock!, disingkat menjadi BTR!, adalah serial manga Jepang oleh Aki Hamaji yang diserialisasikan di Manga Time Kirara MAX sejak edisi Februari 2018. Sebuah Komik Antologi dari serial ini telah terungkap pada Agustus 2022 dan akan dirilis pada Oktober 2022.

Hitori Gotou is a high school girl who's starting to learn to play the guitar because she dreams of being in a band, but she's so shy that she hasn't made a single friend. However, her dream might come true after she meets Nijika Ijichi, a girl who plays drums and is looking for a new guitarist for her band.

Kikuri Hiroi (廣ひろ井い きくり Hiroi Kikuri) is a supporting character from Bocchi the Rock! manga and anime series. She is in charge of the bass and vocals of the band, SICK HACK.

PA-san (PAさん) is an unnamed public address system engineer of STARRY. She dropped out of high school because she could never wake up early and failed too many classes. It's also the reason why she has her current night job.

Seika Ijichi (伊い地じ知ち 星せい歌か Ijichi Seika) is the manager of the live house, STARRY.[7] She is the older sister of Nijika Ijichi.
